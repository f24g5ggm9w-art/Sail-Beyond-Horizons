For Therapists

Let the water do the hardest part of your work.

You know the hardest part isn't the therapy — it's getting people to stay engaged long enough for it to work.

Sail Beyond Horizons is built on a simple insight the research keeps confirming: when the experience is genuinely enjoyable, people show up, and they keep showing up.

In one VA sailing study, 86% of participants completed their treatment program, versus 55% of a matched group who didn't sail — a statistically significant difference in the outcome you care about most.

Your role

After each sailing session, you meet one-on-one with your client to process the experience and translate it into transferable coping skills — locus of control, distress tolerance, psychological flexibility, and more. The metaphors are already in the water; you help the client bring them ashore.

Why therapists join

  • Engaged clients. The sailing does the hard work of getting people in the door and invested.
  • A novel, embodied modality that complements the frameworks you already use — ACT, mindfulness, CBT.
  • We handle the logistics. Boats, instructors, scheduling, and safety are covered by our sail-school partners. You focus on the clinical work.
  • Flexible involvement that fits around an existing practice.

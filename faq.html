FAQ

Questions, answered.

Everything you need to know before your first sail. Don't see your question? Reach out any time.

Do I need to know how to sail?

No. A qualified instructor teaches you everything, and most people are handling the boat comfortably in their first session.

Is this a replacement for therapy or medication?

No. Sail Beyond Horizons is a complementary, skill-building experience that works best alongside your existing care. If you're in treatment, we're happy to work in support of it.

Is it safe?

Yes. Sailing is run by established schools with certified instructors and full safety equipment. Studies of therapeutic sailing have found strong safety records, and comfort on the water is built in from the first lesson.

What if the weather's bad?

Sessions are scheduled around safe sailing conditions. When the water isn't cooperating, we reschedule — reading the conditions is part of the skill.

For Sail Schools

Your boats already change people. Let's make it official.

You've seen it a hundred times: someone steps off the dock lighter than they stepped on. That's not just a good feeling — it's a documented one.

Across 66 hours of therapeutic sailing with participants who mostly carried serious mental health conditions, researchers recorded zero injuries and zero accidents, and 95% described the experience as calm or relaxing.

Sail Beyond Horizons helps you turn what your boats already do into a purpose-driven program — and a new revenue stream — without changing how you teach.

How the partnership works

You do what you already do best: teach people to sail, safely and well. We bring the participants and the clinical side — a network of licensed therapists who run the reflection sessions after each lesson. You keep your boats working; we handle the rest.
